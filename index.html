Welcome to LSA

LSA Rep is a theatre program at Wharton High school that values authentic self-expression within it's cast. Students involved spend countless hours throughout the school year working together to construct shows at a professional level, which are then presented at festivals and events. For Twenty-one-straight-years this team has produced award winning productions, and serves as the District Seat for Statewide Thespian Student Leadership. 

Mission Statement

To inspire out community through the artistic expression of theatre while creating bonds that will last a lifetime.
